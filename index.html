Orange: A Guide to Orange County Beaches

Laguna Beach Huntington Beach Newport Beach

In this blog, I review some of the major beaches in Orange County. California’s coastline is incredibly vast, with dozens of beaches to choose from, and Southern California in particular is famous for its beach culture. Because of that, visiting Orange County can feel overwhelming if you’re not sure where to go for the perfect summer day. This guide breaks it all down by covering what truly makes a beach great: accessibility, parking, crowd levels, cleanliness, swimmability, and the overall beauty of the views and sunsets. I’ll also highlight some of the smaller, lesser-known spots within these beaches that are worth exploring.
